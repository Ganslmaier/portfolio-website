My Story

Thanks for checking out my website! I'm an Munich based UX Designer who previously worked as a Project Manager in IoT Network and UX Design for clients such as Body and Soul, Munich. I am great at create solution for difficult process and I'm at my best when I have the freedom to create order in chaos. Because of my background, I know how to sell an idea and how important a user centered approach is. I love the diversity of UXD: to analyse problems and behaviours in depth and solve problems by being part of the creative process, but always using an objective, analysis backed approach. It's my ambition to always keep learning in life. Open to fixed and freelance opportunities (not limited to Munich) and very much in favour of remote working.
